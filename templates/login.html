Sign in by pasting your iRacing session cookies

iRacing retired its email+password Data API login in December 2025 and has paused issuing OAuth client IDs to third-party apps. Until that reopens, this app needs you to copy the session cookies from a working browser login and paste them below.

How to copy your cookies (≈30 seconds)
  1. Open members.iracing.com and sign in normally.
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools, then switch to the Network tab.
  3. Reload the page. In the request list, click any request whose host is members-ng.iracing.com (look in the “Domain” column, or filter on members-ng).
  4. In the right-hand panel under Headers → Request Headers, find the Cookie: line and copy its entire value.
  5. Paste it into the box below and click sign in.

Alternative: DevTools → Application → Cookies → https://members-ng.iracing.com, then paste them one per line as name=value.

Cookies usually expire after about a day. When that happens, repeat the steps to refresh.

Cookies are stored only in your Flask session on this machine and are sent only to members-ng.iracing.com.
